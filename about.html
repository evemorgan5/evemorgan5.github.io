About

I'm Eve. I'm from Dublin, Ireland. I've been living in San Francisco for the past few years. I have a master's degree in Actuarial Science and an bachelor's degree in Chemical Engineering.

Since moving to the Bay Area, I've embraced the Californian lifestyle and have gotten very into yoga and hiking. Growing up in Ireland you could never depend on good weather for activities outside. It rains sideways!

I volunteer with the outdoor yoga community. They have classes on Baker Beach and it's one of my favorite things to do in the city! If you are interested, definitely come along. I love to read, and tutor kids in a local school in San Francisco how to read. I don't have any pets!
